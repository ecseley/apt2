Non-Chemical Cleaning Machines

HPHW | High Pressure High Water

This technology has now matured into an effective cleaning process without the use of chemicals. Oils are removed from the strip with High temperature water applied at High pressures. A multi stage system harvest the oil and separates it in a stand-by container. The water is filtered, reheated, and recirculated.

  • A method ot remove rolling oil from metal strip using water at high temperture and pressure
  • Non-abrasive, No contact cleaning and degreasing
  • Non-Chemical Cleaning
  • Recircultating System and Continuous Filtering
  • Multi-Stage Oil Recovery
