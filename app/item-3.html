Special Application Machines

S - 8 BRUSH MACHINE

This design locates up to 8 independently operating brush heads on a 7 x 12 ft area. Laid out internally like bridle, foil material is safely brushed due to the large strip support surface.

  • 10 Minute Brush Change
  • Variable Backup Roll Configurations for Cleaning, Satin Finishing, and Graining
  • 2 to 8 Heads
  • Brush process is visible thru internal illumination and large windows
  • Quick brush changes permit use of different brushes fro different finishes
